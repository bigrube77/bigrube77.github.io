Andrew Keenan, Ph.D

I am an instructional designer, researcher, and lecturer. I specialize in innovative approaches for adult education using new media and games. I have a PhD from the University of Toronto in the Faculty of Information. My research focuses on new media, video games, innovative pedagogy, and human-computer interaction. I am currently developing a toolkit for education game design with my colleagues at the University of California, Riverside and the University of Alberta..
