Andrew Keenan, Ph.D

I am an (in alphabetical order) academic, consultant, digital humanities scholar, educator, game designer, instructional designer, lecturer, librarian, project manager, and researcher. I specialize in innovative approaches for adult education using new media, games, and play. I have a Ph.D from the University of Toronto in the Faculty of Information where I studied human computer interaction, information practice, and user experience. I currently work as an Instructional Designer for Yardstick Software in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.
