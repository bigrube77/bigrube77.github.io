Andrew Keenan, Ph.D

I am a (in alphabetical order) consultant, digital humanities academic, educator, game designer, lecturer, librarian, project manager, and researcher. I specialize in innovative approaches for adult education using new media and games. I have a Ph.D from the University of Toronto in the Faculty of Information. My research focuses on new media, video games, innovative pedagogy, and human-computer interaction. I am currently developing a toolkit for education-focused game design in post-secondary institutions with my colleagues at the University of California, Riverside and the University of Alberta.
